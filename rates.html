    Check In & Check Out

    Arrivals & Departures

    • A $2 non-refundable fee is due at time of reservation.
    • 100% payment is due on day of check-in for short-term reservations.
    • 100% payment is due on day of booking for long-term (monthly) reservations.
    • Check-in time is 3:00 pm.
    • Check-out time is 11:00 am.
    • Long-term reservation payments are due in full on the same day as arrival each month. For example, if your arrival date was May 14, your monthly payment would be due on the 14th of every month.

    Refunds & Cancellations

    • A full refund will be given if daily/weekly reservation canceled at least 7 days ahead of your arrival date.
    • A 50% refund will be given if daily/weekly reservation canceled less than 7 days ahead of your arrival date.
    • A full refund will be given if monthly reservation canceled at least 14 days ahead of your arrival date.
    • A 50% refund will be given if monthly reservation canceled less than 14 days ahead of your arrival date.
    • Please give us at least 3 days notice when ending a long term reservation. No fees apply for ending a long term reservation.
    • A partial refund will not be given if you check-out in the middle of a long-term reservation.
